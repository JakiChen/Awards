About Us

ARTalents is a company that represents and promotes talents for any specific requirements of our clients. Our goal is to support the image making community.

Alessandra Ruiu starts scouting and promoting photographers at Studio Ghiglieri, which was one of the first agencies in the sector in Italy.
For over fifteen years she worked with Carla Ghiglieri, certainly one of the most important talents scouts in the field of photography and image, and she acquired a great deal of experience, both human and professional. In recent years Alessandra has had the opportunity to learn the secrets of the trade, has collaborated with leading publishers, designers and advertisers and has seen the discovery of new talent such as Cometti and Mark Borthwick just to name a few. In addition to working with clients to achieve their goals, Alessandra attaches particular importance to “accompanying” Talents in their artistic and professional growth.

Giovanna Ruiu has worked in Rome for over fifteen years in the world of cinema. After a long experience as an agent for scriptwriters and directors, she dedicated herself to her passion, the set, holding various roles until she became assistant director. She has worked alongside Roger Spottiswoode (James Bond) and Wes Anderson (Tenenbaum) and with actors such as Bill Murray, Owen Wilson, Angelica Houston, Isabelle Huppert, Mélanie Laurent, Greta Scacchi, Gigi Proietti and many others. Before joining ARTalents, Giovanna worked for about two years with Carla Ghiglieri at the Paris office, where she had the opportunity to deepen all aspects of this work. In ARTalents Giovanna mainly deals with Production but contributes significantly to all the activities of the company.

ART – Alessandra Ruiu Talents

Is a company that represents and promotes talents for any specific requirements of our clients. Our goal is to support the image making community.

Is also a boutique production agency,  offering small to large-scale production and coordination of editorial and advertising photo shoots, film and digital content creation. Our ambition is to accomplish the creative vision of our clients while providing a high-end service ensuring a smooth and enjoyable production from start to finish: casting, location scouting, stylist, glam team, set designer, equipment renting, technical crew. post production.

ARTalents creates and organizes events and photographic exhibitions in the most exclusive location in Italy.
