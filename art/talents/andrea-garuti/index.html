WHO I AM

Born in Florence in 1965, he became interested in photography at the age of 13, thanks to his father as a photographer. At the age of 17, while studying at the Art Institute of Florence, he pursued his first research in black and white. In the same year he won the prize for best image, awarded by the region of Tuscany. Subsequently he began his studies in Architecture at the faculty of Florence, during his university period, leading to term various reportages, which will be published in some Italian magazines. In the same years he began his experimentation, always in black and white on still life and the city, in these years he linked indelibly to the image in 4×5 with, its Linhof, machine always used also in the commercial work. He works as an assistant for Life, an illusrated sport, alongside David Lees and Cristopher Pillitz. He graduated with honors with a thesis in semiology on the “invisible cities of Calvin”. For a year he became assistant to Prof. Mezzetti with whom he graduated. In 1996 he moved to Milan where he began working with magazines such as Donna and Amica. In the following years he met the Art director Felice Perini, who pushed him towards color research and the to experiment with it in this way.
Between 1999 and 2005 he entered the commercial world in his own right, and launched various campaigns, such as: Etro, Fay Uomo e Donna, Wind telefonia, Libero telefonia, Vodafone telefonia, Terzani Lights, Champion, Phyto, Lierac, Lorenz , Polis Sunglasses, Sting sunglasses, Pesavento jewellery, Oviesse, Testoni At the same time he collaborates with the magazines Amica, Io Donna, Vogue Pelle, Vogue Tessuti, Cosmopolitan, Elle, Elle Decor, Glamour, Houses to Live in, Velvet, Elle Decor UK, Elle Russia, home, Panorama First. Since 2005 he has also taken over the city research, which in 2007 led him to publish his first personal book.

After its publication at the Damiani publishing house, it exhibits in solo exhibitions at: Rome, New York, Milan, Bologna, in group shows in Paris, London, Turin, Genoa. his images are exhibited at the Palais des expositions in Paris, at the art fairs of Bologna, Stuttgart, Verona. He began a new research in 2007 entitled “Cardinal points” which he exhibited during the month of photography in Rome in 2008 and then at the Biennale of Architecture in Turin and then landed at the Auditorium in Rome in May 2010. At the same time he continued his commercial work by shooting campaigns for Trussardi, lottomatica, Caracter occhiali, and began his collaboration with Wall Paper. The research work is now developing in relation to painting and mixing with the language of photography through the transfer on paper and plaster. From 2010 to 2018 he publishes Notebook book with research related to painting, collaborates with Laminam for a book around the world called Atlas Book, book of architecture edited by the company, with presentation at the Architecture Biennale of 2016 in Venice, continues in the production of print campaigns for various brands from Meridiana to Caracter the latest made, collaborates continuously with Elle Decor and Wallpaper, from 2018 begins a continuous collaboration with the study Matteo Thun, for projects in Europe and Italy.
